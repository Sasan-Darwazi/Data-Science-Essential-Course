Whenever you create a plot, your goal is to determine and then communicate the story of your data. At a minimum, this requires clear plot labels and titles. In this module, I will explain how to title, label, and modify your plot to ensure it conveys the message you want to send to your audience.

Note:
 The function xlab() changes the x-axis label,
 the function ylab() changes the y-axis label,
